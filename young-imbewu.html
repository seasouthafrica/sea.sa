Imbewu Future Leaders Programme

Supporting the next generation of South Africa's changemakers.

In this section

  • Social Enterprise Schools
  • International Student Internship Programme
  • Imbewu Future Leaders Programme

South Africa is in the hands of the grassroots youth change-makers, to change the current social and economic crisis. We believe that with our support, these active young citizens can pave the way toward the social, environmental, cultural and economic success of South Africa. Through the principles of "Ubuntu" and passion, coupled with entrepreneurship we can upskill and equip our youth to increase their positive impact.

We will be hosting a Facilitation for Transformation programme, empowering youth to drive positive change impact while creating income-generating opportunities for young leaders to sustain their impact. Participants will be equipped with professional facilitation skills, to be used when convening community groups, speaking with local government officials or working with youth in their environment.

🎤

Why?

  • ✨ Events run by someone with facilitation skills can be transformational.
  • ✨ This means that participants change how they see themselves and their communities.
  • ✨ Just imagine if South Africa's young changemakers were equipped with these skills before they turn 20.

What?

  • 🔷 4-day digital workshop focused on how to achieve transformational learning when leading a group.
  • 🔷 Expertly crafted & customised to the South African context, based on an internationally accredited programme by Glasgow Caledonian University.
  • 🔷 A digital networking event for participants & sponsors.
  • 🔷 Certificates of completion awarded to all participants.

Who?

  • ✅ Internationally renowned facilitators will join from Scotland.
  • ✅ South Africa's best facilitators will join from around the country.
  • ✅ 12–15 young changemakers who are already making an impact in their communities will be hand-chosen by the SEA team to participate.
  • ✅ This programme is led by Social Enterprise Academy Africa, a leader in transformational learning and development in South Africa since 2012.
"In 2022 I attended Facilitation for Transformation. I was selected as the youngest participant with people who had so much experience in facilitation. Now I am using the experience I've gained to impact Wildfire youth in my community church every Friday night. In 2023 I studied fashion design at Fedisa which was always my dream — this was through Social Enterprise Academy because I received certificates that allowed me to get accepted."
— Sikelela Kwatsha, Imbewu Programme Participant
