Partner With Us

Your support plays a vital role in helping us empower social entrepreneurs and build fairer, more sustainable communities across the country.

Partner with us to Maximise Your BEE Scorecard and Drive Social Impact

Social Enterprise Academy South Africa (SEA SA) is a Level 1 BEE organisation committed to empowering social entrepreneurs and fostering sustainable business solutions across South Africa and beyond.

By partnering with SEA SA, your company can make a tangible social impact while significantly improving your BEE scorecard. As a Level 1 BEE entity, companies working with SEA SA can claim 135% of their procurement spend towards their own BEE scorecard — a powerful advantage for enhancing your Preferential Procurement rating.

Additionally, SEA SA is registered to issue Section 18A certificates, enabling corporate partners to claim tax deductions on their contributions. This makes your partnership both socially meaningful and financially beneficial.

Benefits of Partnering with SEA SA

Partnering with SEA SA is more than just meeting compliance requirements — it's about creating sustainable, inclusive growth.
